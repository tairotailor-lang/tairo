Our Story

Tairo was born from a simple observation: Kattankudy has incredibly talented women tailors, but they often struggle to reach customers beyond their immediate neighborhood. Meanwhile, customers face challenges finding reliable tailors who understand their specific needs and cultural preferences.

We created Tairo to bridge this gap. By leveraging the simplicity of WhatsApp ‚Äì a platform everyone in our community already uses ‚Äì we've made it easy for customers to connect with tailors and place orders without any complicated apps or processes.

Today, Tairo is proud to support women tailors in Kattankudy, helping them earn a fair income while working flexible hours from home. Every order placed through our platform contributes to empowering women in our community and preserving the beautiful tradition of hand-tailored clothing.
